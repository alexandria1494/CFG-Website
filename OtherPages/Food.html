Calle Ocho


You aren’t a true New Yorker until you have taken part in the ritual that is Brunch. This place offers a Latin inspired Bottomless Brunch (bottomless means unlimited alcohol for the whole meal, yes UNLIMITED) that regularly features on top brunch lists of NYC. Even though meals are a little pricier (roughly $22) you can sure make the money back by downing those Sangrias!

Remember: this is a local favourite so be sure to book a table ahead of time

Price: $$$

Lombardi’s


America’s first pizzeria … what more needs to be said. The first two things you should know about Lombardi's is that it's cash only and serves whole pies only. Why? That's just the way most of these old-school coal-oven joints are.
So bring cash. And a crew to help you eat.

Price: $$
